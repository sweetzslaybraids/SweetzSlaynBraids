About Me

Welcome to the world of Mz Sweetz's hair braiding & natural haircare zone I was born and raised In Chicago's westSide where I found the inspiration & passion in braiding as a young girl watching celebrity stylists on television excel in their craft. . I then decided to teach myself how to braid and study natural haircare of all cultural hair textures by watching & studying online videos, taking specific online braiding classes as well and drawing clientele at discount prices to have personal contact experiences to perfect my craft. As an adult, I have become a supreme multicultural braider and natural haircare stylist now serving over 300 clients from the East Coast to the West Coast where I enjoy meeting new people and networking with other fellow stylists to learn more to exceed in my craft and expertise. I'm always eager to learn more and keep up with all the new trends and methods currently. I am just a city girl with a bonus personality that matches my passion as a braider. I take all concerns from my clients as if it was my crown which my clients appreciate the most about me. I enjoy making hair grow as well as passing on good information and recommendations to clients to maintain their haircare in between services in my chair. It gives a sense of knowledge to each individual who personally knows where we started from the beginning and tends to give more self-motivation to want to take care of their crown more in-depth. I also enjoy creating hair growth oils that are for all hair including alopecia clients. I overall enjoy the end of every individual visit to see their response to their new look and feel their experience with sweetzslaynbraids
